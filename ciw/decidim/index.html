Mission

Decidim provides a modular and scalable web application to support democratic citizen participation in local governance. Collaboration, transparency, traceability, and information integrity are built-in to its design from its inseption.

Where Decidim Is Being Used

Beginning in the city of Barcelona, Decidim is now being used in Helsinki, Mexico City, and closer to us here in New York City.

From Social to Political Networking

We have become inundated with social networking software designed to mine our behaviors to turn us into marketable commodities. Decidim is part of the effort to build software to empower us rather than rob us, to humanize us instead of dehumanize us.
